Our Beginnings
It was a typical Monday morning. Al was bored out of his mind at his desk job and wanted to escape. The exciting life he envisioned he'd be living after college was further away than Friday night was. With some convincing from old friends, he regained his entrepreneurial spirit from college and decided to open his own steakhouse! Since 2025, Al's Steakhouse has served only the best cuts of meat and has grown a large, loyal fan base. The next time you're in Dupont, craving the best quality steak, come say hi at Al's Steakhouse!
